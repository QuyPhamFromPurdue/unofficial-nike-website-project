ABOUT NIKE

WHY CHOOSE US

Innovation

Among our employees, we're working to ensure representation exists at all levels of the organization, with goals to hold ourselves accountable. As the largest company in our industry, we have a responsibility to reimagine sustainable innovation across the entirety of our value chain.

Our Team

Nike's co-founder and legendary coach—once said, "If you have a body, you are an athlete." We aim to foster inclusion and embrace diversity throughout our business, teams and culture to bring us closer to the consumer, empower our employees to realize their full potential of their ability.

Sustainable Business

We've set bold, science-based targets to reduce our carbon footprint. Our focus is on our biggest impacts: the way we make our products and the way we operate. Our vision is zero waste, period. Chemistry lets us innovate and influence design, performance and sustainability.
